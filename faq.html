COMMON QUESTIONS ABOUT HODE SENIOR CARE

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that choosing home care is a big decision. Here are answers to common questions families ask us.

What makes HODE different?

HODE is uniquely led by a Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) with an MBA in Healthcare Management. This means your care is overseen by a doctoral-level clinician who understands both the medical and management sides of high-quality care.

What does "HODE" mean?

HODE stands for Hope, Outreach, Dignity, and Empowerment. It is also a personal family word meaning "from my core," reflecting our deep commitment to every family we serve.

Do you provide services in my area?

We are proud to serve families throughout Ohio. Please contact us directly to confirm availability in your specific neighborhood.

How do we get started?

Getting started is simple. You can call us for an initial consultation, followed by a professional clinical assessment led by our DNP to create a customized care plan.
