About HODE

More than a name. A promise rooted in love and care.

The meaning of HODE

HODE stands for Hope, Outreach, Dignity, and Empowerment. In our family, “Hode” was a name of endearment between our late mother and father, meaning “from my core” or “from my heart.”

Our mission

Provide compassionate, high-quality home care that promotes dignity, independence, and empowerment for individuals and families who need support at home.

The founders

Tigist

A caregiver and advocate whose family journey shaped the heart behind HODE.

Blen

A Family Nurse Practitioner with a doctoral degree and experience across care settings, focused on practical support that meets people where they are.

Why we started

Families often struggle to find dependable care at the right time. HODE exists to provide clear, respectful support that protects dignity and builds stability at home.

Our mother’s legacy

HODE honors our late mother’s dream that her daughters would build something lasting together. Every family we serve is part of that legacy.
