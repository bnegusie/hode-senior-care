Senior Home Care FAQs

What does "DNP-led care" mean for my family?
It means your loved one’s care is overseen by a Doctor of Nursing Practice. This provides a higher level of clinical expertise, allowing us to better manage complex health conditions and coordinate with primary doctors more effectively than standard agencies.
Do you offer 24/7 care or just hourly visits?
HODE provides flexible care options tailored to your needs. Whether you require a few hours of companionship a week or comprehensive 24/7 support, we build a plan that fits your family's schedule and budget.
How do you select your caregivers?
We select caregivers who share our core values of Hope, Outreach, Dignity, and Empowerment. Every caregiver undergoes rigorous background checks and is personally trained to meet our high clinical and empathetic standards.
Is home care covered by insurance in Ohio?
Home care coverage varies depending on your specific plan and the level of care required. During our free consultation, we can help you review your options and provide guidance on private pay or insurance navigation.
